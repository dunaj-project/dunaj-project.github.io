INumerical

Available since version 1.0 (view source)

not referred automatically

PROTOCOL with method -numerical

A value protocol for objects other than numbers which however have a canonical numerical representation. Types satisfying INumerical protocol must accept their canonical number form in their constructors. Examples of such type is character, instant or UUID.

See also: numerical?, num, Number, number?, dunaj.host.number/number
